# EcoCompute AI: The Green Architect (Project Summary)

**English:**

**EcoCompute AI** is your intelligent "Green AI Architect," powered by **Gemini 3 Pro**. While traditional tools only measure emissions *after* the damage is done, EcoCompute predicts and prevents waste at the code level.

By combining:
*   **Static AST Analysis**
*   **Gemini’s Thinking Budget** (for reasoning)
*   **Google Search** (for live 2026 hardware specs)
*   **Code Execution** (for math verification)

We eliminate hallucinations. It doesn't just list data; it **auto-refactors your PyTorch code** to be up to **50% more energy-efficient**.

We turn Generative AI from an energy consumer into the ultimate energy optimizer.
